"Memories" is a song by American pop rock band Maroon 5. It was released through 222 and Interscope Records on September 20, 2019, as the lead single from their upcoming seventh studio album.

The song was based on "Canon in D" by German composer Johann Pachelbel. It was produced by lead vocalist Adam Levine and production team The Monsters and the Strangerz, and written by Levine, Stefan Johnson, Jordan Johnson, Michael Pollack, Jacob Kasher Hindlin, Jon Bellion, and Vincent Ford. The single peaked at number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.
